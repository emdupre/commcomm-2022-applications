OHBM Communications Committee Applications

The OHBM Communcations Committee (ComCom) aims to provide accessible, actionable information to members of the Organization for Human Brain Mapping through outputs including the OHBM Blog and NeuroSalience podcast. We are actively seeking new members interested in improving their science communication skills, both through interactions within the organization as well as through outreach to the general public.

If you have any questions about this application, please reach out to hello@ohbm-com.com.
